5.5 Sex-linked dominant inheritance

Preceding page

Factors indicating sex-linked dominant inheritance.

  • 1. All female offspring of an affected father will be affected
  • 2. All male offspring of affected fathers will be normal
  • 3. Normal offspring of affected parents produce normal offspring
  • 4. The population frequency in males is lower than in females
  • 5. The segregation ratio is 1:1 in offspring of a normal father and an affected mother
